## Design Context

### Users
Primary users are **shoppers buying perfumes** (storefront-first). The core job is to **discover, evaluate, and purchase** premium fragrances confidently, with minimal friction and a strong sense of trust and exclusivity.

### Brand Personality
- **Personality**: elegant, modern, trustworthy, exclusive
- **Emotional goal**: calm luxury (understated confidence, no “salesy” pressure)
- **Voice**: concise, ritual-oriented, premium; use whitespace and restraint to communicate quality

### Aesthetic Direction
The current UI already signals luxury fragrance (dark, warm gold accents, serif display + modern sans, subtle glow/blur). Going forward, evolve into a **merged direction inspired by**:
- **Collider**: bold editorial sections, strong hierarchy, confident product cards and utility UI patterns (cart/account), clear conversion CTAs without clutter ([Collider](https://www.drinkcollider.com/)).
- **SŌM**: “ritual” language, high-contrast minimalism, dramatic typography scale, premium pacing/scroll rhythm, and a sense of ceremony ([SŌM](https://www.drinksom.eu/)).

**Merged idea for mlm-app (storefront-first)**:
- **Editorial luxury commerce**: product discovery feels like a brand magazine; checkout feels crisp and dependable.
- **Typography-led hierarchy**: large serif display headlines for emotion; restrained sans for clarity and trust.
- **Ritual framing**: microcopy that reinforces routine and personal identity (“discover your signature”, “your ritual”, “member since”).
- **Conversion without shouting**: CTAs are prominent but quiet; avoid “discounty” patterns.

**Theme plan**:
- Support **both** dark and light themes.
- **Light mode** direction: **ivory/cream base + warm gold accents**, keeping the same luxury tone (not stark white).

### Design Principles
1. **Luxury through restraint**: fewer elements, more whitespace, deliberate hierarchy.
2. **Trust is a feature**: clear states (loading/error/success), predictable flows, readable forms, no surprises.
3. **Typography does the heavy lifting**: serif for emotion/brand, sans for utility; consistent tracking and case.
4. **Calm motion, optional delight**: motion can be richer, but always smooth, purposeful, and respectful of reduced-motion preferences.
5. **Storefront-first clarity**: product discovery and purchase flows are always the simplest path; MLM/network features support, not dominate.
